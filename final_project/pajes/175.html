Abai 175th

How to celebrate the 175th anniversary of Abai around the world

On the occasion of the holiday in the capital and in every region of the republic, all cultural events are held in an online format. But at the same time, it should be noted that the celebration of the anniversary of Abai is also taking place on an international scale. Read more about this in the material Caravan.kz . On August 10, at 15.00, the International video Marathon of reading Abai's poems in eight international languages of the world Dubai world challenge (French, English, Arabic, Spanish, Chinese, Japanese, German, Italian) starts. The challenge will be attended by scientists - researchers of Abai's creativity from around the world, translators, writers and representatives of the creative intelligentsia, extraordinary and plenipotentiary ambassadors of the Republic of Kazakhstan and lovers of the great poet's creativity. In honor of the 175th anniversary of Abai Kunanbayev, the US diplomatic mission in Kazakhstan supported the #Abai175 campaign dedicated to promoting the creativity and ideas of the great Kazakh poet Abai in the modern world. This was reported in the press service of the US Embassy in Kazakhstan.

How to congratulate

"Dear Kazakhstanis! I sincerely congratulate you on a special holiday! Today is Abai Day! This holiday is the revival and continuation of spirituality and culture, poetry, and good traditions laid down by Abai Kunanbayuly. We want to celebrate Abai Day at the embassy in the future. As you know, Abai played togyzkumalak.We also plan to hold an annual contest "Togyzkumalak" at the embassy.After that, we will gather at the dastarkhan, in the center of which there will be baursaks and koumiss, we will play the dombra, sing Abai songs, read "Words of Edification" and retell them. Abai's dream is to "bring up a harmonious, integral personality"; this dream is the duty of humanity. This fits the current pandemic situation," said U.S. Ambassador William Moser.

Tokayev – Read more about Abai

The working trip of President Kassym-Jomart Tokayev to the city of Semey began with participation in the opening ceremony of the monument "Uly Akyn Abay Uldarymen". The opening of the composition is timed to the 175th anniversary of the great poet and thinker Abai Kunanbayev. Abai is depicted on the monument with his sons Akylbay and Turagul, NUR reports.KZ with a link to the official website of Akorda
"The Great Abai is not only the pride of our country, but also a genius on a global scale. This is a person who was able to comprehend the full depth of the spiritual world of the Kazakh people. His poems and words of edification deeply reflect our national being. Abai, who is the founder of our modern literature, has left a bright mark in the history of the country. His works have not lost their relevance for more than a century. They still serve as spiritual food for all of us. The poet's rich creative heritage is the heritage of the Kazakh people and all mankind. Abai's precepts are a guideline for the younger generation," Kassym–Jomart Tokayev said.
