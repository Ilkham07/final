Words of edification

Abai Kunanbayev, the great Kazakh poet, philosopher and thinker, left many works, including poems, prose and philosophical works. His work is often filled with deep moral teachings and wise thoughts. Here are a few quotes that can be considered as words of edification from Abai Kunanbayev:

"Be a human among humans, and be a human within yourself."

"In a person, it should not be greatness, but humanity."

"As much kindness as you have, that much you are a human."

"Don't speak ill of yourself. Speak goodness, speak of the good, speak of the beautiful."

"Love is like the sun: it radiates light and warmth into the heart of a person."

"Knowledge brings light to the dark corners of the soul."

"To change the world, start with yourself."

"Live in such a way that you're not ashamed to recall yesterday."
